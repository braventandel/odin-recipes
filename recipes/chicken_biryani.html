Chicken Biryani

About Chicken Biryani

Chicken Biryani is a majestic and aromatic dish that stands as a centerpiece of festive Indian dining. It is a layered delicacy where marinated chicken and basmati rice are cooked separately before being assembled and slow-cooked together in a sealed pot, a technique known as "dum." This process allows the steam to infuse the rice with the complex flavors of saffron, caramelized onions, and whole spices, resulting in a fragrant and hearty one-pot meal.
