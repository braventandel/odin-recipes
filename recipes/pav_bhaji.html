Pav Bhaji

About Pav Bhaji

Pav Bhaji is a vibrant and spicy street food classic originating from the bustling streets of Mumbai. The dish features "Bhaji," a thick and flavorful mash of mixed vegetables like potatoes, cauliflower, and peas, cooked with a special blend of spices on a large flat griddle. It is traditionally served piping hot, topped with a dollop of butter, fresh coriander, and lemon juice, alongside "Pav," soft buttery bread rolls toasted to perfection.
